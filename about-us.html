ABOUT US

Welcome to Esther Cosmetics, your go-to destination for premium makeup and skincare products in Sweden. At Esther Cosmetics, we believe that beauty isn’t just about looking good – it’s about feeling confident, empowered, and caring for yourself in the most sustainable way possible. We are passionate about providing our customers with top-quality products from both well-loved brands and innovative new lines, all while keeping sustainability at the forefront of everything we do. From eco-friendly packaging to cruelty-free and natural ingredients, we are committed to offering beauty solutions that are good for both you and the planet.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bring you the very best in beauty while maintaining a strong commitment to environmental responsibility. Whether you're searching for the latest skincare essentials or the perfect makeup to enhance your natural beauty, we strive to ensure that every product we offer is not only effective but also aligns with our values of sustainability. We believe in customer satisfaction above all else. Your shopping experience is our top priority – from browsing our carefully curated selection to receiving your order with care. We’re here to help you find the right products, offer expert advice, and provide a seamless online shopping experience.

Sustainability at Our Core

As a company based in Sweden, we are deeply inspired by nature’s beauty and the importance of preserving it. That’s why we are dedicated to sustainability in every step of our business – from the products we choose to the packaging materials we use. Our goal is to minimize our environmental footprint, ensuring that beauty today doesn’t come at the cost of tomorrow. We actively work with brands that share our commitment to ethical practices, cruelty-free testing, and using natural or organic ingredients. By shopping with us, you’re not just choosing beauty – you're supporting a healthier planet.
